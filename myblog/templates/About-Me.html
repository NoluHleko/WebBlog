About Me

I am Nolusindiso Hleko. I was born in a farm near a little town called Jamestown in South Africa. So, you could say I am from Jamestown, given that my family eventually moved to the town, and I finished my early schooling years there. In 2010, I moved to Port Elizabeth to study law because I wanted to become a lawyer. After two months I realized that I liked to create more. So, in 2011 I opted to pursue a media degree majoring in Public Relations and went all the way to masters. It was during my first year of masters that I got bitten by the travel bug. In 2017, I moved to Korea to become an ESL teacher and ended up staying in South Korea for 5 years. It is now 2022, I am a full-time student again, Studying Software Engineering, so either I am confused about life, or I really enjoy studying. In this Blog I will be documenting my journey of being an undergraduate student at a university in Germany in my 30s while feeling like I live in 3 different countries: Germany, South Africa and South Korea.
